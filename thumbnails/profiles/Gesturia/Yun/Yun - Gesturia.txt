She has worked on projection design for theater and concert productions touring cities such as Shanghai, Beijing, and Taiwan. Yun has collaborated with international teams in Japan, Singapore, and beyond, contributing to website design, video editing, and user interface development. Her cross-cultural experience has strengthened her communication, teamwork, and design adaptability. With a strong sensitivity to cultural diversity, Yun focuses on transforming global influences into thoughtful, effective design solutions that connect with diverse audiences.
